## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Daily Journal** is a private, secure health journal that helps you track how you feel every day — alongside real health data from your wearable.

### 📋 Daily Check-In
Start each day with a quick wizard that captures:
• Mood (1-10)
• Sleep quality (1-10)
• Medication tracking
• Workload level
• Energy level
• Custom feeling metrics

### 📊 Health Connect Integration
Automatically pull data from your fitness tracker or smartwatch:
• **Step count** for the day
• **Average heart rate**
• **Sleep report** with duration and stages

Health data is fetched from Android Health Connect — compatible with Fitbit, Samsung Health, Google Fit, and more.

### 📝 Markdown Journal
Write free-form journal entries with full Markdown support. Preview your formatted entries or edit the raw text.

### 🔒 Private & Secure
Your health data deserves the highest protection:
• **Biometric lock** — fingerprint or face unlock
• **PIN fallback** — for devices without biometrics
• **Encrypted storage** — all data encrypted with Android Keystore
• **No cloud, no servers** — everything stays on your device
• **No analytics or tracking** — zero third-party SDKs
• **Screenshot protection** — blocked from task switcher and screen capture
• **Auto-lock** — re-locks when you switch apps

### 📅 One Entry Per Day
Each day gets its own entry, combining your check-in, health metrics, and journal in one place. Entries are organized by date and easy to browse.

Daily Journal is completely free, contains no ads, and will never sell your data. Your journal is yours alone.
