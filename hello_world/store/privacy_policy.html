Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Daily Journal ("the App") is a personal health journal application. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

The App collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Manual check-in data: mood, subjective sleep quality, medication usage, workload, energy, and user-defined feeling metrics (entered by you through the daily wizard).
  • Health Connect data: daily step count, average heart rate, and sleep session data (duration and stages). This data is read from Android Health Connect with your explicit permission.
  • Journal entries: free-form text notes you write in the App.

2. How Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted storage (Android Keystore-backed encryption via flutter_secure_storage).
  • The App does not transmit any data over the internet.
  • The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • The App does not have a backend server or cloud storage.

3. Health Connect Data Usage

The App requests read-only access to the following Health Connect data types:

  • Steps — to display your daily step count in your journal entry.
  • Heart Rate — to calculate and display your daily average heart rate.
  • Sleep — to display sleep duration and sleep stage breakdown.

This data is:

  • Used solely for display within the App on your device.
  • Never shared with third parties.
  • Never transmitted off your device.
  • Never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than your personal journal.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete it within the App (swipe to delete an entry) or uninstall the App.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data.
  • The App does not create backups (android:allowBackup="false").

5. Security

  • The App requires biometric authentication or a PIN to access.
  • The App re-locks when you switch away and return.
  • Screenshots and task-switcher thumbnails are blocked (FLAG_SECURE).
  • All data is encrypted at rest using the Android Keystore.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: google@gencan.io
