# Privacy Policy for Daily Journal

**Last updated:** April 20, 2026

Your privacy is our primary concern. This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Journal ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Offline-First and Local Storage
Daily Journal is an offline-first application. **All of your journal entries, settings, and health data are stored locally on your physical device.** We do not transmit, sync, or store your personal data or journal entries on external servers. You maintain complete control over your data.

## 2. Health Connect Integration
Our application integrates with Google's Health Connect to provide enriched journaling features.
- We request **read** access to specific health metrics (such as sleep or activity) to display them alongside your journal entries.
- We do not transmit this health data to any external servers. It is processed and displayed entirely on your local device.
- Our use of information received from Health Connect fully complies with the [Health Connect Permissions policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/11863583), including the Limited Use requirements.

## 3. Data Encryption and Security
To protect your sensitive information, we employ the following security measures:
- **Local Database Encryption:** Your local database is encrypted using SQLCipher.
- **PIN Protection:** Access to the application is secured by a user-defined PIN. We employ brute-force protection to lock the application after multiple incorrect attempts. 

## 4. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking
We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or crash reporting tools that collect or transmit your data.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app is completely offline, any changes will be reflected in an app update provided through the Google Play Store.
